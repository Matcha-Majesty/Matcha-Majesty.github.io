MATCHA MAJESTY

Nana's Green Tea Review

Nana's Green Tea

1250 Broadway, New York, NY 10001, USA

$9.00 S

I ordered this matcha latte unsweetened and with oat milk. The only available milk options were oat, soy or almond. The taste of the oat milk was overpowering and tasted very sweet to where I suspect the matcha is actually a blend that already contains sugar or some sweetener. The matcha itself was of lower quality, creating a slightly chalky after taste in the back of my throat. This matcha may be enjoyable for sweet matcha enjoyers, but those looking for high quality matcha should look elsewhere.
